Since 2019

A boutique built on comfort, craftsmanship & care.

Paw Chic was founded by a small team of designers, veterinarians and pet parents who couldn't find pet essentials that matched the quality — or the look — of the rest of their home. So we made our own.

Today, every collar, bed, bowl and blanket in our collection is designed in our New York studio and produced in small runs by ateliers who share our obsession with detail. We use natural fibers, cruelty-free leathers, and hardware that will still look beautiful years from now.

Because luxury isn't about excess. It's about the small daily rituals — the morning walk, the sunny nap, the shared blanket — done a little more beautifully.
