Home · Privacy Policy

Paw Chic ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

What we gather.

  • Account & order info: name, shipping address, email, and phone number.
  • Payment info: processed securely by our PCI-compliant payment providers — we never store your full card details.
  • Site usage: anonymized analytics that help us improve the shopping experience.

How We Use It

Only what's needed.

  • To process, fulfill and support your orders.
  • To send order updates and, if you opt in, occasional newsletters.
  • To improve our products, site performance and customer experience.

Sharing

We never sell your data. We share only with the trusted partners required to fulfill your order (shipping carriers, payment processors) and where required by law.

Your Rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@pawchic.com. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in every message.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep your cart and preferences working, plus optional analytics cookies you can decline via your browser settings.

Contact

Questions? Reach us any time at privacy@pawchic.com.
