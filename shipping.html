Home · Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Fast, tracked delivery worldwide.

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days from our New York studio. You'll receive a tracking link the moment your order ships.

  • Standard (US): 3–5 business days — $9, or complimentary on orders over $75.
  • Express (US): 1–2 business days — $18.
  • International: 5–12 business days — calculated at checkout.

Returns

30-day, easy and honest.

If a piece isn't quite right, return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be unused and in their original packaging.

  • Start a return through your order confirmation email or by contacting us.
  • We provide a prepaid return label for US orders.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 business days of receipt.
  • Personalized items and grooming products are final sale.

Exchanges

Sizing issues? We've got you.

Exchanges are complimentary within the US. Simply email hello@pawchic.com with your order number and we'll take care of the rest.
